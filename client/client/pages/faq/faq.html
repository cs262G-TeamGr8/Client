How do I create an account?

  • 1. On the home page click the button labeled "Sign up to play",
  • 2. This will take you to a signup page, fill out the required information,
  • 3. Then click the button labeled "Sign Up".

How do I join a team?

  • 1. First log in to the website,
  • 2. Then on any page click the hamburger menu in the top left corner,
  • 3. Then click "Join a Team" in the hamburger menu,
  • 4. This will bring you to a join team page, fill out the required information,
  • 5. Then click the button labeled "Join".

How do I create a team?

  • 1. First log in to the website,
  • 2. Then on any page click the hamburger menu in the top left corner,
  • 3. Then click "Join a Team" in the hamburger menu,
  • 4. This will bring you to a join team page, fill out the required information,
  • 5. Then select the check box if you agree to be responsible for the team,
  • 6. Then click the button labeled "Create".

How do I view my schedule?

  • 1. First log in to the website,
  • 2. Then on any page click the hamburger menu in the top left corner,
  • 3. Then click "My Teams" in the hamburger menu, which will drop down a list of all the teams you are on,
  • 4. Then select the team you wish to view the schedule for,
  • 5. By default all the upcoming games on your schedule are shown.

How do I view the whole schedule for a league?

  • 1. On any page click the hamburger menu in the top left corner,
  • 2. Then click "Leagues" in the hamburger menu, which will drop down a list of all the leagues,
  • 3. Then select the league you wish to view the schedule for,
  • 4. By default all the games in the league, including past games are shown.

How do I view my team's standings?

  • 1. On any page click the hamburger menu in the top left corner,
  • 2. Then click "Leagues" in the hamburger menu, which will drop down a list of all the leagues,
  • 3. Then select the league you wish to view,
  • 4. Then click the button labeled "Standings" to view the standings of the selected league.
